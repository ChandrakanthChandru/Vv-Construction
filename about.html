Founder & Principal Engineer

M. VijayaKumar

B.E. Civil Engineering | 14+ Years of Field Experience

M. VijayaKumar founded VV Construction & Interior Design in 2010 with a clear mission — to bring engineering rigour and creative design together in one firm, serving clients across South India at every budget level.

A graduate of Anna University, Chennai, he has personally supervised over 200 projects ranging from compact city apartments to large-scale industrial facilities at SriCity. His hands-on approach means every client gets direct access to a qualified engineer — not just a contractor.

His work spans Chennai, Bangalore, Delhi, Tada, and the SriCity Special Economic Zone, making VV Construction one of the few firms with proven multi-city execution capability.

Our Journey

14 Years of Building Excellence

From a small Chennai-based team to a multi-city construction and design firm — here are the milestones that shaped VV Construction.

2010

Founded in Chennai

M. VijayaKumar registers VV Construction & Interior Design in Chennai, starting with residential projects in Adyar and T. Nagar.

2013

First Commercial Project

Completes first G+3 commercial complex in Velachery, earning recognition for on-time delivery and finish quality.

2016

Expanded to Bangalore

Opens Bangalore operations, securing IT office interior and renovation contracts in Whitefield and Koramangala.

2019

SriCity Industrial Projects

Awarded first industrial turnkey project at SriCity SEZ — factory fitout for a Japanese automotive components firm.

2022

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Achieved ISO certification for quality management systems — formalising processes across all project types.

2025

200+ Projects Milestone

Crossed 200 completed projects across 4 cities with 98% client satisfaction. Now expanding to Delhi NCR.
